Privacy Policy

Writ - Voice-Powered Legal Drafting for Advocates

Last Updated: December 18, 2024

Our Commitment: Writ is designed with privacy-first principles. Your legal documents and voice recordings are processed locally on your device whenever possible, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Writ ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. Writ is a legal document drafting application designed for legal professionals in India. By using Writ, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Voice and Audio Data

  • Voice Recordings: When you use voice-to-text features, audio is captured temporarily to convert speech to text.
  • Processing: Voice data may be processed using on-device speech recognition (Google Speech Services) or sent to secure third-party transcription services (OpenAI Whisper API) for accurate Hindi/English transcription.
  • Retention: Audio recordings are not stored permanently. They are deleted immediately after transcription is complete.

2.2 Document Data

  • Draft Documents: Legal drafts, templates, and documents you create are stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload your documents to external servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.

2.3 Biometric Data

  • Authentication Only: If you enable biometric lock (fingerprint/face), biometric data is processed entirely by your device's secure hardware (Android Keystore).
  • No Collection: We never access, store, or transmit your biometric data.

2.4 Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version (for compatibility)
  • App crash reports (if enabled by your device settings)
  • General usage analytics (non-personally identifiable)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To provide voice-to-text transcription and legal document drafting features
  • Security: To authenticate users via biometric or PIN protection
  • Improvement: To fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with the following service providers:

  • OpenAI (Whisper API): For enhanced speech-to-text transcription. Only audio segments are transmitted; no personal identifiers are included. OpenAI's data use policies apply.
  • Google Speech Services: For on-device speech recognition when available.

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your documents or legal content with anyone
  • Use your data for advertising purposes

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All documents and drafts are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is protected using AES-256 encryption with keys stored in Android Keystore.
  • No Backups: App data is excluded from cloud backups to protect confidentiality.
  • Secure Transmission: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored within the app
  • Delete: Delete your documents, drafts, and recordings at any time
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable microphone access through device settings
  • Data Portability: Export your documents as PDF files
  • Opt-out: Use on-device speech recognition only (may affect accuracy for Hindi)

7. Data Retention

  • Voice Recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription
  • Documents: Retained until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for up to 12 months

8. Children's Privacy

Writ is designed for legal professionals and is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Permissions Explained

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required for voice-to-text dictation features
  • Internet (INTERNET): Required for enhanced speech transcription and fetching court cause lists
  • Biometric (USE_BIOMETRIC): Optional, for app lock feature
  • Storage: To save and export PDF documents (uses scoped storage on Android 10+)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011
  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
