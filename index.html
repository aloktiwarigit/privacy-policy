1. Introduction / परिचय

LegalAdvocate Pro ("App", "we", "us") is a legal practice management application designed for legal professionals in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

LegalAdvocate Pro ("ऐप", "हम") भारत में कानूनी पेशेवरों के लिए डिज़ाइन किया गया एक कानूनी अभ्यास प्रबंधन एप्लिकेशन है। यह गोपनीयता नीति बताती है कि हम आपकी जानकारी कैसे एकत्र, उपयोग, संग्रहीत और सुरक्षित करते हैं।

2. Information We Collect / हम कौन सी जानकारी एकत्र करते हैं

2.1 Information You Provide / आपके द्वारा प्रदान की गई जानकारी

  • Case Information: Case numbers, party names, FIR details, court names, hearing dates
  • Legal Documents: Draft applications, petitions, and legal documents you create
  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings when using speech-to-text features
  • Private Notes: Court practice notes and hearing preparation notes
  • Settings: App preferences, language settings, PIN for private notes

2.2 Automatically Collected Information / स्वचालित रूप से एकत्रित जानकारी

  • Usage Data: Features used, documents created (anonymized counts only)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility)

3. How We Use Your Information / हम आपकी जानकारी का उपयोग कैसे करते हैं

Purpose / उद्देश्य Data Used / उपयोग किया गया डेटा
Generate legal documents Case details, voice input
Speech-to-text transcription Voice recordings (sent to OpenAI Whisper API)
AI-assisted formatting Legal text (sent to Anthropic Claude API)
Store your cases locally All case information
Protect private notes PIN, encrypted notes

4. Data Storage & Security / डेटा संग्रहण और सुरक्षा

4.1 Local Storage / स्थानीय संग्रहण

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your case information.

आपका सभी डेटा आपके डिवाइस पर स्थानीय रूप से संग्रहीत है। हमारे पास ऐसे सर्वर नहीं हैं जो आपकी केस जानकारी संग्रहीत करें।

4.2 Encryption / एन्क्रिप्शन

  • All local data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored in Android Keystore / iOS Keychain
  • Private notes are additionally protected by PIN with PBKDF2 hashing

4.3 Security Measures / सुरक्षा उपाय

  • Data encrypted at rest
  • PIN protection for sensitive notes
  • No cloud backup of case data
  • Secure API communication (HTTPS)

5. Third-Party Services / तृतीय-पक्ष सेवाएं

We use the following third-party services for AI features:

5.1 OpenAI Whisper API (Speech-to-Text)

  • Data Sent: Voice recordings for transcription
  • Purpose: Convert Hindi/English speech to text
  • Retention: OpenAI may retain data for 30 days for abuse monitoring
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

5.2 Anthropic Claude API (Text Formatting)

  • Data Sent: Legal text for formatting and review
  • Purpose: Format Hindi legal documents, grammar correction
  • Retention: Anthropic does not train on API data
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
Important: When using AI features, portions of your legal text are transmitted to these services. Do not include highly sensitive information in AI-processed sections.

6. Data Sharing / डेटा साझाकरण

We DO NOT:
  • Sell your personal data
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Create user profiles for marketing
  • Store your data on our servers
We MAY share data:
  • With AI service providers (as described above) only when you use AI features
  • If required by law or court order

7. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023) / आपके अधिकार

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

Right / अधिकार Description / विवरण
Access View all data stored in the app
Correction Edit or update your information
Erasure Delete all your data from the app
Portability Export your data
Withdraw Consent Stop using AI features anytime

How to Exercise Your Rights / अपने अधिकारों का उपयोग कैसे करें

  • View Data: Settings → Data Management → View Data
  • Export Data: Settings → Data Management → Export All Data
  • Delete Data: Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data
  • Withdraw AI Consent: Settings → Privacy → Disable AI Features

8. Data Retention / डेटा प्रतिधारण

Data Type Retention Period
Case Information Until you delete it
Private Notes Auto-deleted after 3 years if not verified
Voice Recordings Deleted immediately after transcription
Usage Statistics 30 days (local only)
